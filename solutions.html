APEX Agent

Stays on watch after the scan

A scan is a snapshot. The Agent keeps watching everything you track and remembers your holdings the next time you ask a question.

Proactive monitoring

Re-scans your watchlist and portfolio, surfacing score drops, new red flags, and sector-relative shifts — each with a concrete research checklist, not just a ping.

Compare-on-alert

When a red flag fires, the Agent attaches a factual one-line comparison to the most relevant peer — not the flag in isolation.

Cross-holding patterns

If two or more holdings trip the same flag in one run, that's one consolidated "shared exposure" notification — not separate pings.

Concentration monitoring

Tracks sector concentration across your combined watchlist and portfolio, and nudges you when it's lopsided.

Weekly digest

One connected narrative each week — score trend, watchlist changes, movers, and upcoming earnings — not a pile of separate bullets.

Custom watch conditions

Set your own bounded rule — "alert me if the P/E crosses 35" — and the Agent watches it for you.
