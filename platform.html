Under the hood

One score, six explainable parts

The overall score breaks down into named sub-scores, each with a plain-English reason grounded in sector-benchmark data — not a black box.

0–10 scale

Profitability

Margins and return on equity, benchmarked against sector medians — not one flat bar for every industry.

0–10 scale

Financial Health

Balance-sheet strength — debt/equity, current ratio, and liquidity — each read against sector norms.

0–10 scale

Valuation

How the price compares to earnings, growth, and sector peers right now.

0–10 scale

Risk (Volatility)

How much the stock actually moves, and what that says about downside risk.

0–10 scale

Momentum

Recent price and fundamental trend — improving or deteriorating.

0–10 scale

Red Flags

How many — and how severe — of the specific risk checks below are currently triggered.

Scores are benchmarked against the sector Finnhub's classification actually maps a company to — not a single universal threshold applied to every company regardless of industry.
